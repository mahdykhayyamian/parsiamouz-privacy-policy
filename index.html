Privacy Policy for ParsiAmouz

Last updated: August 9, 2025

ParsiAmouz respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

Information We Process

When you use certain features (such as speech-to-text or text recognition), the app sends:

  • Voice recordings for speech recognition
  • Images for text recognition

These are sent directly to Google’s APIs for processing. We do not store this data ourselves—it is used only to provide the requested feature and is handled according to Google’s Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

ParsiAmouz does not store your voice or image data. Google may temporarily process and store this information as described in their privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API
  • Google Cloud Vision API
